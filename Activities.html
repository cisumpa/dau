CURRENT PRIORITY: “Building a Community Centre for Transformative Education and Digital Inclusion Project in Dzaleka Refugee Camp”


SUMMARY OF ACTIVITIES ACHIEVED

1) General activities consisted in a large outreach animation program that would help set up a community restoration program in Dzaleka community, With the help of community animators trained in 2017-2018, animations were conducted in 9 villages (Karonga, Blantyre, Katubza, Zomba, Likuni I, Likuni II, Lisungwi, Kawale, and Majengo Mapya). The animations were followed by the formation of village committees of 9 women focal points who will work hand in hand by DAU Animators to identify all cases in their respective villages and refer those cases to DAU office; so that DAU would listen and refer them at the appropriated level in the camp for further assistance. These teams are effective because, they are chosen within the community and they are women, also refugees and therefore understand the refugee environment, language, challenges and culture, they know about both the home country and the host country. The members of the community know and trust our focal points or animators. What is very necessary is constant capacity building for all DAU members and it is the only weapon that enables us to move forward and afford to contribute to the changes we have reached here

2) Our building of hope Family Clinic was an answer that met the high level of cry of beneficiaries and entire refugee and host community, which has enable DAU to solve all health problems including: all reproductive health issues, family planning, all sexually transmitted infections and HIV/AIDS, as well as some GBV cases are provided with full care. a. We promote girls’ education by paying them school fees and various school needs. all are the fruits of Hope Family Clinic b. Trainings, workshops with various topics on different groups in the community, Accelerating Women’s Economic Empowerment and life skills are all the fruits of Hope family Clinic. c. Raising their voices on the issues of women and girls through awareness campaign, debates, tribune sessions, claiming their rights and so on all the fruits of Hope family clinic

3) The construction of small office and Listening house or women and girls safe space is a great benefit to our work
