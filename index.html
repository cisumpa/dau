Welcome to Dzaleka Azimai Union!

Let's Raise together our voices for women and girls.




It is time for the charitable organizations,international community individuals ( like you ) to recognize the injustice of denying refugee girls and women an education.

MISSION

Empowering lives, restoring rights and Upholding women’s Dignity.

VISION

To build a society in which all kind of women and girls have access to Fundamental right and opportunities to decide about their body, future and community life in order to achieve their full Potential for development

VALUES STATEMENTS

In order to lead the process of change, our work is guided by the key values, such as our solidarity with the community we serve, this means: Loyalty, compassion, commitment, teamwork and valuing each other.
